This is how testing works

Place an order

Either online or over the phone you can get a full 8 panel test or individual STD tests.

Visit a nearby lab

We have over 4,000 labs across the country with visits can take less than 15 minutes.

Get results.Fast.

Within 1-3 business you review your results either online, by email or through SMS.

Private & Confidential

HIPAA laws prevent us from sharing results with anyone other than you. Confidentiality is a must before, during and after you get tested for HIV and other STDs.

Certified Advisors

Free pre & post test consulting by our nurses and counselors. Treatment options available for anyone testing positive.

Payment Options

We accept all major credit cards, prepaid credit cards, health savings account, cash deposits, money orders. We also have payment plans available, get tested now pay later.

Go in today

No appointment needed, just call or order online to recieve your verification number then go test. Please keep in mind that hours vary throughout our network. No exams needed.
